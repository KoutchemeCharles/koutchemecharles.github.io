Me and Computer Science

Computers really gained my attention back in high school, at 11th grade, when I started learning how to code. I have always enjoyed understanding how things work. The initial reason why I decided to pursue a career in computer science was to be able to stay up to date with all the new cool technologies. But computer science is way more than that. It is an inter and multidisciplinary field which open many doors. It allows one to acquire the knowledge necessary to have an impact in any other domain: physics, medecine, sports, games, etc. The possibilities are endless. I am really glad I decided to follow this track. In our modern society, computer science knowledge gives us a superpower. I want to use my skills to contribute to the development of innovative initiatives and build technologies that will serve. I believe technology is all about sharing. By sharing our knowledge, we enable people, communities, and organizations to thrive, create, and contribute in their turn. The growth is exponential, and the reach is endless. What we build today can have an impact far beyond our expectations.
